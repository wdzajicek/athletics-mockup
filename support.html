Support KCC Athletics

The Kankakee Community College Athletics programs give students the opportunity to develop athletic skills at the same time they pursue academic success. This establishes a foundation of physical and mental well-being that student-athletes can build on for a lifetime.

You can make a difference in a student-athlete's life with your generous contribution to KCC Athletics. Your donation will help keep these opportunities open, giving our student athletes their best chance to achieve athletic and academic success.

There are a variety of ways you can contribute:

Cavalier Club

The Cavalier Club promotes and supports Kankakee Community College athletic programs and encourages the involvement of the entire Community College District 520. The club sponsors fundraising events each year. Proceeds help support all KCC athletic programs:

  • a fall membership drive
  • the Cash Bash held in February

You can be a member of the team! Your support helps build and sustain athletic programs at KCC. No matter your level of giving, you are contributing to the development of champions--on the field, in the classroom, in the community.

Membership levels and benefits

Platinum – $1,000 and ​above

  • 10 season passes to all KCC athletic events
  • Business listed in team guide, KCC website and gymnasium
  • KCC fleece and hat
  • FREE Cash Bash Ticket

Gold – $500

  • 8 season passes to all KCC athletic events
  • Name listed in team guide and KCC website
  • KCC polo and hat

Red – $250

  • 5 season passes to all KCC athletic events
  • Name listed in team guide and KCC website
  • KCC hat and T-shirt

Blue – $100

  • Two season passes to all KCC athletic events
  • Name listed in team guide and KCC website
  • KCC hat

For more information about the Cavalier Club, phone Todd Post, athletics director, at 802-8616 or email tpost@kcc.edu.
